Araku Valley Attractions

- A Journey to the Heart of Nature's Beauty -

Katiki Waterfalls

A beautiful cascade surrounded by lush greenery, perfect for nature lovers.

Lambasingi

A serene hill station known for its cool climate, scenic landscapes, and lush greenery, often referred to as the 'Kashmir of Andhra Pradesh.

chaparai Waterfalls

A picturesque waterfall surrounded by rocky terrain, perfect for nature walks and a refreshing dip.

Ananthagiri Hills

A peaceful retreat offering scenic views, dense forests, and serene trekking trails.

Coffee Plantation

Explore the lush, aromatic fields where the finest coffee beans of Araku Valley are grown.

Kollaput Jala Tarangini Park

A tranquil park featuring mesmerizing water features and a perfect spot for relaxation and nature walks.

Borra Caves

A stunning limestone cave system known for its impressive stalactites and stalagmites formations.

Padmapuram Gardens

A beautifully landscaped garden offering a serene environment and unique tree-top huts for a tranquil escape.

Galikonda Viewpoint

A breathtaking vantage point offering panoramic views of the rolling hills and lush valleys of Araku.
